Classic Vanilla Cake

Ingredients

    Cake:

    • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 1 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
    • 1 cup water
    • 1 tablespoon vinegar
    • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

    Frosting:

    • 1/2 cup butter, softened
    • 2 cups powdered sugar
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 2-3 tablespoons milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat to 350°F (175°C). Grease an 8-inch pan.
  2. Mix Batter: Combine dry ingredients. Add wet ingredients and mix until smooth.
  3. Bake: Pour into pan and bake for 25-30 minutes. Cool completely.
  4. Make Frosting: Beat butter, add powdered sugar, vanilla, and milk until smooth.
  5. Frost Cake: Frost cooled cake.
