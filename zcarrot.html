Healthy Carrot Cake

Ingredients

    Cake:

    • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 cup granulated sugar
    • 1 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 2 cups grated carrots

    Frosting:

    • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
    • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
    • 2 cups powdered sugar
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat to 350°F (175°C) then grease an 8-inch pan.
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. After that, add oil, eggs, and vanilla; mix until combined. Fold in grated carrots.
  4. Pour batter into the pan and bake for 25-30 minutes. Let the cake cool completely.
  5. In a bowl, beat cream cheese and butter until smooth.
  6. Gradually add powdered sugar and vanilla, beating until creamy.
  7. Spread frosting over the cooled cake.
