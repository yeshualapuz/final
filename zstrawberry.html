Creamy Strawberry Cake

Ingredients:

    For the cake:

    • 1 box strawberry cake mix
    • Water, oil, and eggs (as per cake mix instructions)

    For the filling and frosting:

    • 2 cups fresh strawberries, chopped
    • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
    • 1 and 1/2 cups heavy cream, chilled
    • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Follow the instructions on the box of strawberry cake mix to prepare the cake batter.
  2. Pour the batter into a greased 9-inch round cake pan.
  3. Bake according to the package instructions.
  4. Let the cake cool completely.
  5. Mix chopped strawberries with granulated sugar in a bowl.
  6. Let it sit for about 15 minutes to release juices.
  7. In a mixing bowl, beat chilled heavy cream, powdered sugar,
    and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  8. Once the cake has cooled, spread the strawberry filling evenly over the top.
  9. Then, spread the whipped cream frosting over the strawberry filling.
  10. Chill the cake for a bit if you prefer.
  11. Slice and serve!
